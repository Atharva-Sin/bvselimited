    Fully automatic autoclaves are offered with unique single lever lock for lid. autoclaves are offered in different sizes from 15 to 300 liters. These Fully Automatic Autoclaves have customized microprocessor based control system which ensure required sterilization accuracy, repeatability & safety.
    Applications:
    • Suitable for a wide range of applications wherein fast and assured sterilisation is required.
    • Ideal for Pharma QC, QA, Micro, R&D, Chemical Plants, Bio-Labs, Water & Air Pollution Labs, Fisheries, Mineral Water Plants, Food, Beverage & Distilleries, Hospitals, Nursing Homes & Speciality Doctors.
    Advantages:
    • CE Certified
    • Unique single lever lock for lid with single hand opening.
    • Microprocessor Based Control ensures repeatability & a high accuracy.
    • Conformance to National & International Standards.
    • Low water detection.
    • Spring loaded/reptured disc safety valve for over pressure.
    • Pressure interlock on door.
